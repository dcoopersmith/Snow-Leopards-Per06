Snow Leopard Challenges

Countries Where Snow Leopards Are Endangered

Although snow leopards have ruled the mountains of Central Asia for thousands of years, recently their numbers are dropping in the following countries:

  • China
  • Bhutan
  • Nepal
  • India
  • Pakistan
  • Afghanistan
  • Russia
  • Mongolia

Causes of Snow Leopard Population Decline

Many factors contribute to their declining numbers. Some of the top causes are:

  1. Climate change
  2. Local communities
  3. Killed by farmers
  4. Human settlement in their range
